A Journey through Books

Books I've loved and ones I'm about to explore

Goodnight Beautiful - Dorothy Koomson

A moving and suspenseful story about childhood friendship, betrayal, and the devastating consequences of keeping secrets.

The Couple at Number 9 - Claire Douglas

A gripping psychological thriller where a young couple uncover chilling secrets buried in the garden of their new home.

I Know What You've Done - Dorothy Koomson

A twist-filled domestic noir that explores the secrets behind a seemingly perfect neighbourhood.

Atomic Habits - James Clear

A powerful non-fiction guide on how small changes in daily routines can lead to remarkable life transformation.
